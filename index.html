How It Works:
3 Stage Process

Step 1: Relocation Strategy Session

We begin with a private session to assess your eligibility, answer key legal questions, and outline the best visa path based on your profile — D7 or D8.

Step 2: Personalized Legal Plan & Full Application

We design a legal relocation plan tailored to your nationality, income, and goals. Then we handle every step: document preparation, certified translations, and submission.

Step 3: Legal Setup & Relocation Support

Before your visa application, we secure your Portuguese NIF, open a local bank account, and help you meet all pre-visa requirements — including suitable housing. Once your visa is approved, we continue supporting your integration: from tax guidance to residency registration.
